MEET OUR DOTA 2 ROSTER

HARD CARRY

Double King

Niels Derek Cossio

At 17 years old, Double King has taken the scene by storm, quickly becoming one of NA Dota's most exciting players to keep an eye on. Known for his mature and calm demeanor, Double King is a force to be reckoned with this season.

MIDLANE

Mark

Mark Ng

Full of energy in and out of the game, Mark is a vocal and dominant force amongst NA's new blood. In his free time Mark seeks more competition in the form of tennis and martial arts.

OFFLANE

Luis

Luis Echevarria

Peruvian Offlaner Luis has plenty of experience in DPC coming from the SA region, and is looking to make his mark in NA. Hitting 10k MMR shortly after joining the team, Luis is hungry for more success.

SOFT SUPPORT

AfrOmoush

Mohammad Eis

Bringing some veteran experience to the team, AfrOmoush has played Dota professionally in three different regions. The captain looks to conquer NA DPC after previously playing for The Cut and 2Based.

HARD SUPPORT

Sanctified

Tucker Towsley

Hailing from DPC Team 5ManMidas, Sanctified has had a very quick journey to the top of NA Dota. Just two years ago Sanctified was 4K MMR, eager to get to the top, he is already playing amongst NA's best.
