CDUB is here to stay, and we want to share this journey with you. We are searching for partners, fans, and liked-minded gaming enthusiasts to work with and push the CDUB brand to its fullest!

WHY ESPORTS?

As the world turns more and more towards the digital landscape, Esports continues to explode in growth. More fans, enthusiasts, companies, and money are in Esports more than ever before, with Covid-19 further pushing the boundaries of how people spend their time. Esports is no longer just a passion project for enthusiastic CEO's, over 2200 companies publicly announced their Esports sponsorship deals in 2021 (per Nielsen Sports).

As you can see from the global fan growth chart (per Syracuse University), money and sponsors aren't the only thing growing in Esports! Esports fans are passionate, dedicated, and extremely loyal to their favorite teams. Most of the fan growth in this industry comes from the accessibility and simplicity of viewing the games, anyone can get hooked and easily watch the games for free on their streaming website of choice!

WHY US?

While we are extremely passionate and have fun behind the scenes, CDUB is dead serious on becoming a lasting pillar of North and South American Esports. Surrounded with people that have real Esports experience, we pride ourselves on understanding the dire sacrifices and commitment that is required from an organization in this industry.

In our Dota 2 debut, we absolutely proved our capabilities as a brand and organization. CDUB had the highest peak viewership of ANY North American Division 2 team with 20000 viewers in our game versus DogChamp. That staggering number is 225% greater than the average viewership in Division 2 NA matches. With that result coming from our first event, we can only imagine what the future holds for us.
