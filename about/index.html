ABOUT CDUB

In 2020, CDUB Esports was in its early stages of development and creation, finally releasing in May 2022 with CEO Otto "timbits" Prax at the helm. As a young and grassroots organization, we have simple goals and values: find players and members with as much passion and love for Esports as us, and provide them with the best support we possibly can.

We ventured into our first Esport by acquiring Dota 2's "Happy Seals" who previously played in the North American Dota Pro Circuit Division II. While our initial ambition was to be a competitive force in professional Dota, that has not stopped us from venturing out towards other gaming opportunities like Age of Empires. We hope we can share this amazing journey with you, so stay tuned and keep your eye on CDUB!
